ABOUT

Sustainable Architecture & Living Spaces

This website is a curated collection of references around sustainable materials in architecture. It focuses on how low-carbon, renewable, and durable materials shape the design and performance of residential buildings. Through images, articles, PDFs, videos, and audio, it examines specific sustainable materials. This includes bamboo, hempcrete, rammed earth, mass timber, reclaimed wood, cork, straw bale, recycled steel, and emerging bio-based systems like mycelium, following their use from concept to construction. See more here.

This website also serves as a resource for knowledge, inspiration, and appreciation of sustainable architecture and living spaces. Explore a variety of pages featuring real-world examples and material-focused content to help inform and inspire your next residential project.
